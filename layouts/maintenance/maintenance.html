Antenna Tuning


When environment conditions of the user change, either related to their lifestyle or country issues, the biofield resonance can be suppressed, reducing the BSQ effect.
BSQ tuning allows the user to fix the suppression without purchasing a new device.

Antenna exchange


In some cases, the biofield signal is not only suppressed, but also out of phase and consequently the antenna calibration becomes an ineffective solution.
The antenna swap allows the user to reconnect with the technology, without the need to purchase a new device.

Hardware Repair


As an electronic device, the BSQ has many parts that can break or disconnect after long-term use.
We offer long term support for all of our products, by providing advise to replace the power source locally, or instructions if you need to send it back for repairs.
